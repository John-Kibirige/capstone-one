Mental Health Global Summit

The Mental Health Global Summit is a world-class forum for you to collaborate with national and international mental health leaders, while gaining tools to improve your approach to client care.

The summit brings together the community of experts and academicians from all over the world

This year we hope to expand our invitation list including organizations and individuals who want to work with us on shared projects that advance th cause of our organization, free culture and open knowledge.

So if you are active and engaged in the worlds of open content and knowledge related to mental health we hope you will consider joining us this year to build a stronger more vibrant mental health free society together

Please contact us per Email for any further questions about PLC Global Summit 2022! plcsummit2022@us.org
